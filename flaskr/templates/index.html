Our Mission Statement

With the future of irreversible, world-wide climate change approaching in the current state of the world, everyone has a responsibility to do their part to be more sustainable. However, trying to commit to this can be less than optimal without the know how, the tools or the motivation to accomplish this.

That’s where (yo)UB Sustainable comes in! By “gamifying” how an individual can accomplish sustainability in a way that’s fun and realistic for them, we transform sustainability from a chore into a challenge with rewards.
